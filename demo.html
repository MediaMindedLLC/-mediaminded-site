Inbounder

Instantly processes new inbound leads. Triggers speed-to-contact workflows within seconds of lead creation.

Agent Dialer

AI voice agent calls leads using natural conversation. Powered by Vapi for human-like appointment setting.

LP Caller

High-volume outbound dialer for Lead Portal campaigns. Automated scripts with live transfer capability.

Column Runner

Systematically works through CRM pipeline columns. Re-engages dormant leads on configurable schedules.

Audience Runner

Builds targeted lead segments for campaigns. Filters by product, source, engagement score, and geography.

Listener

Real-time webhook monitor. Watches for CRM events, form submissions, and API calls to trigger automations.
